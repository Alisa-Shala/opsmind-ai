# Duplicate Invoice Policy

Duplicate invoice risk means that an invoice may have been submitted more than once. This can happen because of vendor resubmission, system errors, manual entry mistakes, or repeated invoice numbers.

Duplicate invoices should be placed on hold before payment. Accounts Payable should verify:
- Whether the invoice ID or invoice number already exists.
- Whether the same vendor submitted a similar amount.
- Whether the invoice has already been paid.
- Whether the invoice was resubmitted after a rejection or delay.

Duplicate invoice flags are high-priority because they can lead to double payment and financial loss.
