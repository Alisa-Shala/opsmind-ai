# Three-Way Matching Policy

Three-way matching is a control process used before invoice payment. It compares three documents:

1. Purchase Order: confirms that the company authorized the purchase.
2. Goods Receipt or Service Confirmation: confirms that the product or service was received.
3. Invoice: confirms the amount requested by the vendor.

If the purchase order is missing, the invoice should not be approved automatically. If the invoice appears to be a duplicate, it should be placed on hold. If the invoice amount is unusually high or different from expected values, it should be reviewed by Finance Controller or AP Manager.

Three-way matching helps prevent unauthorized payments, duplicate payments, and incorrect invoice amounts.
